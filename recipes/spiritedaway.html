Chihiro's Onigiri or rice ball

Description

Feeling down? A little overwhelmed about your parents who, against your petition, devoured random food in an empty restaurant without waiting for the server or paying and turned into pigs? Have a bite of this rice ball and all your despair will disappear.

Ingredients

  • A bowl of cooked Japanese rice, fluffy but not too fluffy.
  • A filling of your choice - traditional choice would be umeboshi, or pickled plum
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Lightly season the rice with salt.
  2. Grab a chunk of the rice and form it into a triangle with a divot for the filling.
  3. Place the filling in the divot. Cover it with the rice and mold it into a triangular shape.
  4. Voila! (FYI, it works better as a mood-lifter if you're as hot as Haku.)
