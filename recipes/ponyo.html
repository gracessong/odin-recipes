Ponyo's Ramen with ham

Description

Ponyo loves ham. Ponyo loves ramen. And you should too.

Ingredients

  • Instant ramen noodle soup package
  • Hot water
  • Sliced ham
  • Hard-boiled egg
  • Green onion for garnish

Steps

  1. ...Really? You're cooking an INSTANT ramen. Sure, whatever. Place the dry block of noodles in a bowl with the seasoning and powder soup base.
  2. Pour boiling water over it. Let it sit.
  3. Place the ham, egg, and green onions on top of the noodles. Serve after 3 minutes and while hot.
