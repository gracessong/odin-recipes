Howl's bacon and eggs

Description

When it comes to the most important meal of the day, you should treat yourself right with this easy-to-make, impossible-to-fail recipe for a plate of juicy bacon and eggs.

Ingredients

  • Bacon. Thick, thin, maple-syrup-infused, smoked - whatever you want.
  • Eggs. The rounder the better.

Steps

  1. Pour a generous amount of oil in the pan and bring it up to high heat.
  2. Bacon or egg, which goes in first? Well, clearly Howl chose the bacon first and I think he's very much correct. In whichever order you please, carefully place your ingredients in the pan.
  3. Cook them to perfection and serve. See? Impossible to fail.
